## Who We Are

Sax & The City is a saxophone learning resource for beginners and intermediate players. We cover everything from your first notes to improvisation, gear reviews to music theory -- across jazz, pop, funk, classical, and R&B.

Our mission is simple: help you find your voice on the saxophone, one article at a time.

## Our Approach

Every article on this site is designed to be practical and actionable. We reference real musicians, real recordings, real gear, and proven teaching methods. We want you to finish reading an article and immediately have something to try in your next practice session.

## About Our Content

We believe in transparency. Our articles are generated with the help of AI (specifically, Anthropic's Claude), then reviewed for accuracy and musical correctness. We use AI as a tool to produce consistent, well-structured content, but the knowledge behind each article is grounded in established saxophone pedagogy, real method books, and the teachings of respected musicians and educators.

You can read more about how we create and review our content on our [Editorial Policy](/sax-and-the-city/editorial-policy/) page.

## Who Is This For?

- **Complete beginners** who just picked up a saxophone (or are thinking about it)
- **Returning players** getting back into it after a break
- **Intermediate players** looking to level up their technique, tone, or improvisation skills
- **Players of all styles** -- jazz, classical, pop, funk, R&B, and everything in between
